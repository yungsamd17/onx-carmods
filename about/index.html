About

ONX Vehicle Mods is a regularly updated List of all Vehicle Mods seen on the ONX FiveM Roleplay Server. ONX is a gaming community with a focus on roleplay. Offering multiple services for roleplayers to create in a story-driven, immersive environment. And lastly, FiveM is a modification framework for Grand Theft Auto V enabling you to play multiplayer on customized dedicated servers such as ONX.

About XX% of the Mods are the same as seen on ONX. And the other XX% are either exclusively made by the ONX team or acquired from other sources, and or mod creators.


If you found a mod that needs to be Added, Removed, or Updated Make an Issue about it here.
